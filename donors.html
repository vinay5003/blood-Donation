Donation Process

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

Registration

We’ll sign you in and go over basic eligibility.

You’ll read some information about donating blood.

We’ll ask you for your complete address.

Visiting

We will book a slot at your preferred donation center.

You visit the donation center.

Our staff will check your details.

You are ready to donate the blood.

Donation

The final step.

You donate the blood

You save a life.

After donating the blood our staff will ask you take rest for sometime


Requirements by blood donation type

These are some of the requirements donors must meet to be eligible to donate blood based on their donation type.

  • You must be at least 16 years old in most states
  • You must weigh at least 110 lbs
  • You must be in good health and feeling well**
